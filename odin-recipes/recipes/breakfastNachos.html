Breakfast Nachos

Description

As summer vacation comes to a close, let's make breakfast memorable while we still have lazy mornings to kick back and relax.

This play on steak and eggs features tortilla chips loaded with your favorite nacho toppings, seared flat iron steak, scrambled eggs, and an herb-packed chimichurri sauce.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound flat iron steak
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried rosemary
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried marjoram
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried coriander
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried dill

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F.
  2. Season the steak with the salt, pepper, garlic powder, onion powder, smoked paprika, cumin, chili powder, oregano, thyme, rosemary, marjoram, coriander, and dill.
  3. Heat the olive oil in a cast-iron skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Sear the steak for 3 minutes on each side.
  5. Transfer the skillet to the oven and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the steak reaches an internal temperature of 135°F.
  6. Remove the steak from the skillet and let it rest for 10 minutes before slicing.
  7. While the steak rests, prepare the chimichurri sauce.
  8. Combine the parsley, cilantro, garlic, red wine vinegar, olive oil, salt, and pepper in a food processor.
  9. Pulse until the herbs are finely chopped.
  10. Scramble the eggs in the same skillet used to cook the steak.
  11. Assemble the nachos by layering the tortilla chips, scrambled eggs, sliced steak, and chimichurri sauce.
  12. Serve immediately.
